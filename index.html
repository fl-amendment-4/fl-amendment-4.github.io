Amendment 4, which restores felon voting rights upon completion of sentence, was passed by Florida voters in 2018.

In 2019 the Florida Legislature passed Senate Bill 7066 which claimed to implement Amendment 4, and specified what terms of a sentence must be satisfied as well as avenues for waiving or converting fines and fees to community service hours.

This site is a repository of the documents about Amendment 4, Senate Bill 7066, and the court cases challenging the legislature's effort to implement Amendment 4.
